Privacy Policy

Kribu.io is committed to privacy-first design across all our products and services. This means:

  • We collect the minimum data necessary to deliver our AI solutions and advisory services.
  • We use encryption in transit and at rest wherever possible.
  • We never sell personal or corporate data to third parties.
  • For enterprise clients, GenAI-enabled modernization services respect corporate governance and compliance requirements.
  • Transparency: we clearly communicate what data is used, and why.

As Kribu.io evolves, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect new products (such as the personal assistant) and new advisory services. For questions, contact us at privacy@kribu.io.
